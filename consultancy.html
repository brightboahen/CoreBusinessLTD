Educational Consultancy



Higher Education Institutions in the UK work in an extremely competitive environment. It is important to achieve favourable returns on extensive recruitment investments and annually reach a desired application volume.
Institutions now realise the importance of information they need in order to make best strategic recruitment decisions and in-country representation in order to achieve their goals through efficient spending. Generating knowledge about student market through research and analysis is vital nowadays and universities invest more into pro-active marketing tools in countries of their interest.

Previous experience working for one of the leading universities in London equipped us with necessary knowledge and understanding of the admissions process, mandatory documents, an importance of high-quality well-thought through personal statements and good references required for successful acceptance on a course.

There are several existing educational consultancy companies in Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan, however, marketing is at very poor levels and neither of them have a representation in the UK.

Core Business offers international students a wide-range of consultation services:

  • University and course selection
  • Assistance with admissions process
  • Educational counselling
  • Proof-reading of statements and applications
  • Application submission courier services
  • Preparation for an interview
  • Online English language classes
  • Pre-departure services
  • Possible search of student accommodation
  • Student airport pick up upon request
  • Ancillary training and development during studies in the UK
  • Job search upon graduation

International students can fund their studies through savings, family support and loans. They can also secure grants from their home government to pay for fees and living costs. Scholarships are available in most of the universities and, therefore we can assist in applying for scholarships.
We are determined to maintain the values and ethical standards on which our company was founded. Therefore, we are unable to write personal statements on behalf of students in order to secure scholarships. However, we are happy to give advice and guide students through the application process in order to make sure that all mandatory documents are in order and personal statements meet the requirements.
