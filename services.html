Academic Integrity Programme



We offer a unique training course on Academic Integrity, which discusses types of academic dishonesty including cheating and plagiarism and teaches students the principles of academic dishonesty.

Uniqueness of this training programme is in the approach. We aim at changing students' perception of the problem and demonstrating the serious consequences of academic dishonesty. The programme also covers tutorials on time, stress and pressure management skills as keys to high academic achievements.

The Academic Integrity is already being practised at Higher Education Institutions in the UK. However, we propose a Peer Education principle which is more successful and efficient in educating students, and directly involves them in the delivery of the programme.

In an informal setting and using an interactive approach, students will learn the ways of avoiding cheating and plagiarism and maintain the academic standards. Students will benefit in many ways as they will develop important skills such as presentation and public speaking, leadership and communication.

Our unique training programme will enhance and complement existing training programs institutions offer to their students.
Potential benefits for Higher Education Institutions include but not limited to:

  • Potential decrease of plagiarism and cheating cases
  • Improved awareness of students
  • Unique training programme
  • Student involvement
  • Marketing benefits
